Signal at Dawn

The archive records that at 05:12 UTC the AXIOM listening post on Luna received a repeating burst. The modulation did not match any known civilian or military signature.

"Command, this is Node-04. We have pattern, twelve cycles so far. Recommend escalation to ORIGIN map."

Analysts marked the event as the first verified contact with the Sigma lattice. Further orders were relayed to stations within AXIOM to enter Protocol Amber.
